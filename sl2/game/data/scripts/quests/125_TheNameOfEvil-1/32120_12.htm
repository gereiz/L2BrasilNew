Balu Kaimu:
I Balu Kaimu, a pillar of wisdom, which means in the language of the Elroki "eyes watching the sea." You have pronounced the word Schillen to wake me up!
Now you znaeete how to communicate with me. Tell me, how did you know the word? It gives a sacred link between my mother Schillen and ancient wars Elroki ...
"I am a traveler came here to help the tribe Elroki. They call me tonight."
